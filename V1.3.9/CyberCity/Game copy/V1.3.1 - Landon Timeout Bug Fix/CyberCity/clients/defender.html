Welcome to CyberCity!

You are the Defender, and it’s your job to protect the city from being taken over! A sneaky hacker is on the loose, so you’ll need to take action and keep each location safe from being compromised. If a location's compromise level is greater than or equal to 75, it can only be restored using Intrusion Detection. However, if the compromise level reaches 100, the location is permanently lost to the Hacker. The game has 10 rounds, and each round has 2 turns. You’ll take both turns in the first round to set things up, and the Hacker will get the last round to try and take over. In between, you’ll both take turns each round. At the end of round 10, points will be calculated to determine the winner. Each location will be worth 1 point, except Lackland will be worth 2 points. Use your budget wisely and save the city!
